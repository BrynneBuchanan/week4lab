Welcome to French Lick, Indiana

Nestled in the rolling hills of southern Indiana, French Lick is known for its historic charm, mineral springs, and resorts.

West Baden Dome

The West Baden Springs Hotel boasts a massive free-span dome, once the largest in the world.

Larry Bird

Basketball legend Larry Bird hails from French Lick, and his legacy inspires many young athletes here.

Polar Express

During the holidays, the French Lick Scenic Railway transforms into the magical Polar Express experience.

My Connection

I grew up in French Lick and have countless fond memories of exploring the trails, attending sports games, and working as a Polar Express Elf during winter. This small town taught me big lessons.
